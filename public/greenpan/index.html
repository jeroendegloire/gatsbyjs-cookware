In 2007 GreenPan introduced healthy ceramic non-stick cookware into the market. It was the start of a worldwide cookware revolution. Unlike traditional non-stick cookware GreenPan doesnt use any toxic chemicals. Its Thermolon coating is made with natural materials, such as sand, and doesnt contain PFOA, PFAS, lead or cadmium. The beautiful design and high quality materials result in a unique cooking experience. Its the perfect brand for passionate chefs who want to cook healthy without compromising on performance!
